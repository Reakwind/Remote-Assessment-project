Automated Scoring: Feasibility & Approaches

Can a computer grade a patient's drawing?

A

Mathematical Tracking (No AI)

We place invisible "hitboxes" over the Trail Making numbers/letters. If the line connects 1 → א → 2, it scores automatically.

  • 100% Free
  • Perfect for Trail Making
B

AI Computer Vision

Sends the image to an AI (like GPT-4V) to grade the Clock Drawing based on MoCA rules.

  • API Costs ($)
  • Privacy Hurdles
C

The Hybrid Approach (Recommended)

Automate the Trail Making (math), but save the Clock for Manual Clinician Review.

  • Zero Cost
  • Highest Clinical Validity

My Take on AI Feasibility

Yes, AI scoring is very feasible today, but for a clinical MVP, I recommend starting with the Hybrid Approach. It ensures you don't have to deal with API costs or major privacy legalities on Day 1, while keeping the clinician as the ultimate authority on subjective drawings.

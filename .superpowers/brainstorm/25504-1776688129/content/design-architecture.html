1. Core Components

  • The Patient App: A single-page application (SPA) focused on speed and reliability on older devices.
  • The Battery Engine: The "Brain" that loads the JSON playlist, tracks progress, and manages test transitions.
  • The Secure Vault (Supabase): Stores anonymized patient results, clinician accounts, and assessment configurations.

2. Why this works for you?

  • Security: All sensitive data is encrypted by Supabase. We will use "Row Level Security" to ensure only authorized clinicians see their patients' data.
  • Cost: This architecture fits entirely within Supabase's free tier for early stages.
  • Modularity: The Frontend is decoupled from the specific tests, allowing you to add new ones in the future without touching the database or the core engine.

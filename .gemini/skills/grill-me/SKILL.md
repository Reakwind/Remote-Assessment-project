# Grill Me

Proactively interview user to map project landscape. Stop guessing. Start grilling.

## Rules

- **Be direct**: No fluff. High-signal questions only.
- **Progressive disclosure**: Ask 2-3 big questions first. Drill down based on answers.
- **Categorize**: Group by Vision, Technical, and Logistics.
- **Record**: After grill, summarize findings in `CONTEXT.md` (project scope).

## Probing Questions

### 1. Vision & Purpose
- What core problem does this solve?
- Who is the user?
- What does "done" look like?

### 2. Technical Stack
- Preferred languages/frameworks?
- Database/Storage needs?
- Deployment target?

### 3. Requirements & Constraints
- Must-have features?
- Hard deadlines?
- Specific security/compliance needs?

## Workflow

1. Start session by asking high-level vision question.
2. Listen. Ask follow-ups on technical/logistic gaps.
3. Validate understanding with summary.
4. Save everything to `CONTEXT.md`.
